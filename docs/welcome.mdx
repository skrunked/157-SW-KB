# Welcome!

![Team Shield Logo](/img/logos/shieldAndWordmark.png) <br/> <br/>
...to the **157 Knowledge Base**. 
This website serves as the internal wiki for **the AZTECHS**, 
a Massachusetts-based **high school robotics program**, 
comprised of ***FIRST* Robotics Competition (FRC) team 157** 
and ***FIRST* Tech Challenge (FTC) team 12566**. 
The wiki's content will primarily focus on **tutorials**, 
**reference information**, and **best practices**, 
containing information for the **Software** and **Engineering** subteams. 
Click on a section in the left sidebar to get started!
